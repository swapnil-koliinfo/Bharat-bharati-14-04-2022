Bharat Bharati Trust

Bharat Bharati is an apolitical and non-profit organisation working for the cause of national integrity, cultural unity and harmony among various socio-linguistic diaspora of India. Such goals are pursued through organising various socio-cultural programs by the organisation, on regular basis since more than 17 years in Surat, Gujarat and in other major cities of India.

Apart from pursuing these virtuous goals we also work for the cause of education and health, the two basic needs of the society. At the time of natural calamities and health emergencies, Bharat Bharti comes to centrestage and undertakes programs to serve the suffering humanity irrespective of their caste, creed, religion, region etc

In order to achieve the goals concerning National Integration, every year we celebrate Republic day with difference and organise Raksha Bandhan program with a different tone, texture and style. We organise Bharat Mata Pujan and Bharat Mata Aarti, national Food Festival and Mega Cultural events show casing cultural specialties of more than 25 states of India. During special Raksha Bandhan ceremony, sisters of one state tie Raksha Sutra to brothers of other states and this way we endeavour to strengthen and celebrate the spirit of National Unity.

During last 17 years of its influential existence, Bharat Bharati, while focusing on its goals has organised various Mega events participated by dignitaries and glitteraties like Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi, cine stars, sports personalities, central ministers, and personalities of international repute etc.
